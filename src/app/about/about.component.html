ABOUT

PHANTASMAGORICS is a design studio.
We are focused on Interiors+: Full-scale interior renovations, site-specific interior installations, and the finishing and furnishing of interios. We also design outdoor environments of linked interior spaces. Our work is user-tailored, narrative-drivven, heritage-informed, and easefully modern.

We reshape and reconfigure space.

Employ engaging textures and alluring materials.

Implement clever color and creative lighting.

Curate sound and scent.

Source and design captivating objects and furnishings.

Structured via schematic and design development, construction documentation and administration - we creativespaces that are artful, sometimes droll, always transformative, and entirely fulling. We are based in New York City.
