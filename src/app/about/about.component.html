PHANTASMAGORICS is a design studio that focuses on transformative renovations, bespoke installations, finishings and furnishing at interior spaces, as well as their associated exterior environments: Interiors+.

Collectively structured via Schematic and Design Development, Construction Documentation and Administration, we:

Reshape and recast spaces.

Employ engaging textures and alluring materials.

Implement clever color and creative lighting.

Curate sound and scent.

Source and design captivating objects and furnishings.

Our work is user-tailored, narrative-driven, heritage-informed, and easefully modern.
